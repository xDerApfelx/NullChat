👋 Welcome to NullChat

NullChat is not like Discord or WhatsApp. There are no accounts, no servers, and no message history. Everything is peer-to-peer — you connect directly to the other person.

To get started, share your Peer ID with a friend. They paste it in their app, click Connect, and you're in. Both people need to be online at the same time.

Check the Help section (top-right corner) for tips and explanations. Found a bug? Open an issue on GitHub.

Take a look at Settings to set up your microphone and customize shortcuts.
